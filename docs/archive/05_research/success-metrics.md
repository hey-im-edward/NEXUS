## GO/NO-GO Decision Points

### Week 4 (POC Complete):
**GO IF:**
- 3/5 testers would use it
- Technical foundation solid
- You're confident to continue

**PIVOT IF:**
- <2/5 testers interested
- OR users say "nice but don't need"
- OR too many technical blockers

---

### Week 8 (MVP Complete):
**GO IF:**
- 10+ active users
- Positive feedback majority
- Clear use cases emerging

**PIVOT IF:**
- <5 active users
- Feedback: "don't need this"
- No retention (people sign up but don't return)

---

### Week 12 (Iteration Complete):
**GO IF:**
- 20+ active users
- Users referring others
- 1-2 paying customers
- Clear product-market fit signals

**PIVOT IF:**
- <10 active users
- No organic growth
- Users not willing to pay

**STOP IF:**
- <5 active users
- Majority negative feedback
- No signs of traction after 3 months
